## How to use these

- Treat colors/fonts above as a **starting palette** — refine for modern contrast & accessibility (WCAG AA).
- Keep the **logo** and one or two signature brand colors; modernize the rest.
- Draw on the captured **layout structure** (screenshots + component map) for IA, not pixel styling.
- Define a proper scale (type, spacing, radius, shadow) in `tailwind.config` + shadcn theme.
- Add micro-interactions/animations the original likely lacks (hover, scroll reveal, transitions).
- Cross-reference `pages/<id>/*.png` screenshots for composition inspiration only.
