# Positioning & Messaging Strategy

How to *message* the site persuasively — the angle, not a literal feature dump. Use this in the copy (Mission 03) and the homepage/funnel build.

## Angle

A trusted, expert, easy-to-work-with company that delivers responsive service, quality work, and reliable results.

**Audience:** Prospective customers evaluating the company's services and looking for a credible, responsive provider they can contact easily.

## Emphasize (lead with these)

- trust
- expertise
- ease of working with the company
- responsiveness
- quality
- results
- clear next steps
- social proof
- certifications
- case studies and project examples
- accessible contact options

## Avoid saying directly (reframe instead)

- jargon-heavy messaging
- high-pressure sales language
- dated visuals
- overwhelming long forms
- reusing old imagery

**Tone:** professional, confident, warm, clear

Primary conversion goal is to turn visitors into qualified leads. Every major page should guide visitors toward low-friction contact or quote requests.
